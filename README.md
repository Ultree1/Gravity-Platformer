Overview

Gravitas is a 2D puzzle-platformer where the player uses a gravity gun to manipulate the direction of gravity on various objects. Each room presents a unique physics-based challenge, requiring the player to think creatively to progress through the futuristic testing facility.

Features 
• Gravity-altering gun that can flip or redirect gravity on specific blocks 
• Physics-based environmental puzzles • Interconnected level rooms with increasing difficulty 
• 2D side-scrolling movement and platforming 
• Light background music and sound effects

Genre & Style • Genre: Puzzle-Platformer
